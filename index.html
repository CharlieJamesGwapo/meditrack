How It Works

Getting started with MediTrack takes less than a minute. Follow these four simple steps.

Step 1

Scan QR Code

Scan the clinic QR code or use your personal patient QR to begin your visit or booking.

Step 2

Book Appointment

Choose your preferred doctor, date, and time slot. Instant confirmation sent to your account.

Step 3

Visit Clinic

Arrive at the clinic and check in using your QR code — no paperwork, no long queues.

Step 4

Get Records

Access your medical records, prescriptions, and visit history online or print them anytime.
