Simple Process

How It Works

Getting started with OPD takes less than a minute.

1

Scan QR Code

Scan the clinic QR code or use your personal patient QR to begin your visit or booking.

2

Book Appointment

Choose your preferred doctor, date, and time slot. Instant confirmation sent to your account.

3

Visit Consultation

Arrive at the clinic and check in using your QR code — no paperwork, no long queues.

4

Get Records

Access your medical records, prescriptions, and visit history online or print anytime.

Scan to Book Online

Quick Access

Book in Seconds

Point your phone camera at the QR code to instantly open the OPD booking page. No app download required — just scan and book your appointment from anywhere.

  • Works on any smartphone camera
  • No login required to scan
  • Opens directly to the booking form
  • Available 24/7 from anywhere
Why Choose Us

Why OPD?

Built for patients and clinic staff alike — simple, fast, and secure.

Fast Check-In

Skip the front-desk queue. Scan your personal QR code upon arrival and you're checked in instantly — letting staff focus on your care.

Secure Records

Print Anytime
